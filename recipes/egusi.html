Egusi Soup

Description

Egusi(melon) soup is a popular Nigerian delicacy made with melon and vegetables and eaten with pounded yam, fufu or eba.

Ingredients

  • Melon
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Scotch Bonnet
  • Pumpkin Leaf
  • Bitter Leaf(washed and squeezed)
  • Palm Oil
  • Cow Beef
  • Fish
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Cook the beef with curry, thyme and seasoning cube. And also steam the fish.
  2. Blend the tomatoes, scotch bonnet and onion to form a paste. Then slice and wash the Pumpkin leaf.
  3. Place a pot on a cooker, pour in some palm oil and leave to heat up but not very hot and pour in your paste.
  4. When the paste becomes thicken, add in your washed bitter leaf and stir to incorporate. Next, add the melon and stir then leave for 3mins. Repeat this process thrice.
  5. Add the beef with its stock, the fish and allow cook for 20mins.
  6. Finally add your salt and seasoning, then allow to cook 10mims and the food is ready.
