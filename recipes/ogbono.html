Ogbono Soup

Description

Ogbono Soup also known as draw soup is a common delicacy among the Western, Eastern and Southern people of Nigeria. It is usually consumed with either pounded yam, fufu or eba.

Ingredients

  • Ogbono(grounded)
  • Palm Oil
  • Pumpkin Leaf
  • Cow Beef
  • Pepper(grounded)
  • Smoked Fish
  • Stock Fish
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the cow beef till soft, then wash the smoked fish and the stock fish.
  2. Slice the Pumpkin leaf, wash it properly and set aside.
  3. Place a pot of water on a gas cooker, leave to boil then add the pepper and palm oil and stir properly.
  4. Next add the ogbono and stir properly to mix it and to prevent formation of lumps.
  5. Add the cooked beef, smoked fish, stock fish and allow to cook for 15mins.
  6. Finally add the salt and Seasoning Cube and allow to cook for 10mins. . . Your soup is set for consumption.
