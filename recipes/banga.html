Banga Soup

Description

Banga(palm oil) soup is a popular dish among the southern people of Nigeria in the West African continent. It is made from mashed palm kernel fruit, cooked with fish, beef and periwinkle and can be eaten with starch or eba.

Ingredients

  • Palm kernel(Banga)
  • Cow Beef
  • Fresh Fish
  • Periwinkle
  • Crayfish
  • Refined Spice
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Grounded Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the cow beef for 30mins and steam the fish, then set aside.
  2. Wash the palm kernel into a cooking pot and boil on a gas cooker for 40-50mins
  3. Sieve the water from the kernel, then mash with a pestle in a mortar.
  4. Wash the mashed kernel with warm water, sieve the oily liquid into a pot.
  5. Place on a gas cooker, add the grinded pepper and spice and allow to cook for 10mins
  6. Next add your cooked beef, Crayfish and periwinkle and allow to cook for 30mins.
  7. Finally add the fish, salt and seasoning cube. Leave to cook for 15-20mins after which the soup is ready.
